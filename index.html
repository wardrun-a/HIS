She just worked with her unknown magical gift which made him reconnect to this world. and he is sure that he can achieve a lot for the one that he thinks that the purpose of his life to make her happy and not to let her eyes spit a single drop of tear.


And i wish all the best and blessings for him to achieve what he thinks that is HIS.
